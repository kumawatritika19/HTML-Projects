Our MISSION is to provide quality insurance security and related services to businesses, individuals and publics institutions, and be a dependable long-term steward of the trust that policyholders, shareholders and other important stakeholders place in us.

Success Comes from Focus and Staying Power

Old Republic traces its beginnings to 1923, although several acquired subsidiaries began operations much earlier. We are primarily a commercial lines underwriter serving the insurance needs of a larger number of organizations,including many of North America's leading industrial and financial services institutions.

Our subsidiaries actively market, underwrite, and provide risk management services for a wide variety of coverages, mostly in the general and title insurance fields. The breath o coverages ensure wide diversification and dispersion of risks. Additionally, Old Republic's companies focus only on carefully selected major sectors of the North American economy that are not uniformly exposed to the same business cycles. Old Republic operates in a decentralized manner that emphasizes specialization by type of insurance coverage, industary, and economic sector. Old Republic's general insurance bussiness ranks among tha nation’s 50 largest, while our title insurance business is the third largest in its industry.

Old Republic os one of the nation's 50 largest shareholder-owned insurance businesses. We are a number of the Fortune 500 listind of America's largest companies.
